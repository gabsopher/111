Frequently
Asked
Questions

What do I need to bring?

You should bring seating, blankets, bug spray, and
possibly a friend!


Can I bring my pet?

No, sorry. Fluffy friends aren’t allowed in Bulldog
Park.


Is there an age limit?

5 years old and up is allowed.

How do I know if I’ll like the music?

You can look at the genres of music that each
specific band plays and try to estimate your
preference.

How many people are allowed in?

150


Is there a curfew?

Not downtown, but you will be asked to leave
Bulldog Park by 2 AM.

What type of food is available?

The concession stand has candy, pretzels,
nachos, soft drinks, and water until 1 am.


Can I bring a snack?

You can’t bring in any outside food or beverage.


How do you audition?

Go to the Lineup page and fill out the form at the
bottom of the page.


What will happen at the audition?

You will perform a setlist of 3 to 4 songs to a
group of 3 judges.



Any unanswered questions?
Please email gabsopherdesigns1@gmail.com.
