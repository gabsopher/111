Wish



Genre:
Rock


Hometown:
Merrillville, IN


Band members:
Zack Stanley, Neil
Simon, Mike Jones
and David Massat



Wish is a rock band from Northwest
Indiana. The band was formed in 2001
as a musical outlet among friends.
Nowadays, Wish has evolved into
businessmen, fathers and husbands.
But the group still enjoys performing
their eclectic mix of sounds and
atmospheres. As performers, Wish
loves to put on a show and get the
audience going. They may be old now,
but they can still rock the stage.
