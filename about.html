ABOUT
THE EVENT

EVENT

11-1 is located at Bulldog Park in Crown Point,
Indiana this concert offers a brand new experience!
Local musicians will perform from 11 pm to 1 am. You
might know some of the musicians since their
hometowns are all nearby! The music genres vary so
be sure to check the schedule to check who will be
performing before you arrive. The concessions will
be available for purchase until midnight. We are still
in need of musicians for June 27th to September 14th
so if you or someone you know is interested in
performing please sign up to audition!

ABOUT THE
SPONSOR

The City of Crown Point Official Planning
Committee organizes multiple events throughout
the city. They sponsor various types of events
including the Lake County Fair, The Corn Roast,
The Car Show, and The Farmers Market. The
committee is funded by local donors to entertain
the community. To join or donate money please
visit www.crownpoint.in.gov/.
